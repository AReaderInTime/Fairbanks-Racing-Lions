About Us

Fairbanks Racing Lions has a long history of serving the Interior. Since 1986 Fairbanks Racing Lions have promoted motorsports safety and provided a fun and educational environment for racing. We have secured 175 acres of property between Fairbanks and North Pole for a future motorsports complex. The complex will include courses for year round racing: drag racing, road races, mud and snowmachine drags and snowcross.

Join Fairbanks Racing Lions!

Fairbanks Racing Lions is always looking for new team members. We are an easy going club of like minded individuals working to make a different in our community.

Drop into one of our monthly meeting to see what we are all about. No membership is necessary to attend a meeting.

To join Fairbanks Racing Lions - simply complete the application and submit your dues at the next membership meeting or mail to the address on the application. Annual dues is $70 and starts July 1st. Dues can be prorated.
